Secret Recipe:



Guess The Name of The Famous Turkish Dish!

HINT: It was one of the matching cards!

INGREDIENTS

* 2 tbsp olive oil

* 1 onion , sliced

* 3 green peppers, sliced

* 3-4 medium chopped tomatoes

* 5 eggs

* 2 garlic cloves, crushed

* salt



INSTRUCTIONS

1-Heat the oil in a heavy-based frying pan. Stir in the onions, garlic and pepper. Cook until they begin to soften. Add the tomatoes, mix well. Cook until the liquid has reduced, season.

2-Using a wooden spoon, create 5 pockets in the tomato mixture and crack the eggs into them. Cover the pan and cook the eggs over a low heat until just set.

3-Sprinkle some salt, and black pepper preferably.
